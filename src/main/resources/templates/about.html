About Us

About Us: Experience Newari Hospitality

Welcome to Newa Chen, where Newari traditions come to life in every detail. Nestled in the heart of Kathmandu, our Newari-themed hotel offers an immersive experience that celebrates the richness of Newari culture, cuisine, and hospitality.

Our Story

At Newa Chen, we believe that hospitality is more than just accommodation; it's a journey of cultural discovery. Inspired by the centuries-old Newari heritage, our establishment was born out of a passion to provide guests with an authentic experience that captures the essence of Nepal's history.

A Fusion of Tradition and Comfort

Step into a world where modern comfort meets Newari traditions. Our thoughtfully designed rooms and suites are adorned with intricate woodwork, handcrafted artwork, and warm hues that reflect the spirit of the Kathmandu Valley. Each corner tells a story, inviting you to unwind and connect with the soul of Nepal.

Culinary Delights

Indulge in the flavors of Newari cuisine at our on-site restaurant. From the timeless flavors of Yomari to the savory delights of Bara, our menu is a culinary journey through Nepal's most beloved dishes. Our chefs, trained in the art of Newari cooking, bring a piece of heritage to every plate.

Your Newari Home Away from Home

Whether you're here for leisure or business, Newa Chen offers an escape from the ordinary. Immerse yourself in Newari traditions through cultural programs, workshops, and guided tours. Our attentive staff is dedicated to ensuring your stay is nothing short of extraordinary.

Embrace the Newari Experience

We invite you to be a part of our Newari journey. From the moment you step inside Newa Chen, you'll be greeted by the warmth of our culture and the comfort of modern hospitality. Discover the charm of Nepal, create lasting memories, and embrace the vibrant tapestry of Newari traditions.

Visit us at Kathmandu, and let Newa Chen be your gateway to an unforgettable Newari-themed escape.
