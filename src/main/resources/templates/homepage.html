Newari Dishes

Newari Set

All the dishes together represent celebration and festivity to the Newar people. The Samaya Baji symbolizes good luck, fortune, health and longevity

Choila

Choila is a famous and desired appetizer served together with Baji (beaten rice).Choila meaning 'Roasted Meat' is one of the most prominent dishes in Newari culture of Nepal from ancient time.

Bara

Thick savory pancakes made from lentil paste mixed with spices such as asefoetida, cumin, turmeric, and ginger, which together give the bara flavor beyond just a simple batter.

Yomari

a steamed fish-shaped dumpling covered in new-harvest rice flour and filled with chaku (molasses) or khuwa (condensed milk) and sesame seeds.

About Us

Welcome to Newa Chen, where Newari traditions come to life in every detail. Nestled in the heart of Kathmandu, our Newari-themed hotel offers an immersive experience that celebrates the richness of Newari culture, cuisine, and hospitality.

At Newa Chen, we believe that hospitality is more than just accommodation; it's a journey of cultural discovery. Inspired by the centuries-old Newari heritage, our establishment was born out of a passion to provide guests with an authentic experience that captures the essence of Nepal's history.
